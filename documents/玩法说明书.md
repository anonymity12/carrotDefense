## ğŸ® Controls & Deployment

### 1. Deploy Police Units
1.  Select a unit from the **Operations Menu** at the bottom (Auxiliary, Traffic Cop, Patrol, SWAT).
2.  Hover over the icon to see stats (Damage, Range, Speed).
3.  Click on an **empty grid space** to deploy.
4.  *Note: Deployment costs Budget (Coins).*

### 2. Promote Units (Upgrade)
1.  Click on a deployed unit.
2.  Click the **Blue Upgrade Button**.
3.  Upgrading improves enforcement efficiency (Damage) and monitoring range.
4.  Max Rank is 3.

### 3. Dismiss Units (Sell)
1.  Click on a deployed unit.
2.  Click the **Red Trash Button** to dismiss the unit and recover partial budget.

### 4. System Controls
*   **Pause/Play**: Top right controls.
*   **Abort/Restart**: Reset the current operation.
